How I Approach SEO Projects

A look at the process behind the work — detailed case studies with client data and screenshots available on request.

PROJECT TYPE

Local service business — GBP & map-pack optimization

Home services niches (HVAC, plumbing, flooring, fencing, pest control)

The challenge: Local service businesses competing for map-pack visibility against established competitors with stronger review profiles and citation history.

What I did: Audited and optimized Google Business Profiles, resolved NAP consistency issues across directories, restructured location and service pages around search intent, and built out locally-relevant on-page content.

Tools used: Google Business Profile, BrightLocal, Google Search Console, Screaming Frog

PROJECT TYPE

eCommerce & SaaS — technical SEO audit

Multi-niche eCommerce and SaaS accounts

The challenge: Sites with crawlability, indexing and Core Web Vitals issues limiting organic growth despite decent content.

What I did: Ran full technical audits covering crawl budget, indexation, structured data, canonicalization and JavaScript rendering issues, then prioritized fixes by impact and coordinated implementation with developers.

Tools used: Screaming Frog SEO Spider, Google Search Console, PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix

PROJECT TYPE

Content & topical authority build-out

Legal, finance and EdTech content strategy

The challenge: Thin, keyword-stuffed content that wasn't building real topical authority or matching search intent.

What I did: Built topical maps and content clusters using entity and EAV-based coverage, wrote content briefs aligned to search intent, and structured internal linking to distribute authority across topic clusters.

Tools used: MarketMuse, SearchAtlas (OTTO SEO), SEMrush, Ahrefs

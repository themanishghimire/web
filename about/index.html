About Me

MY STORY

Rooted in Nepal, built on how search engines actually think

I'm Manish Ghimire, originally from Tansen, Palpa, and now based in Balaju, Kathmandu. I hold a Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) from Ratna Rajyalaxmi Campus, Tribhuwan University, which gave me the technical foundation, databases, web development, systems thinking, that now shapes how I approach SEO.

Over the past two years at RankMeTop, I've grown from SEO Intern to SEO Team Lead, working across 50+ projects spanning legal, SaaS, eCommerce, home services, finance and more. Along the way I built a strong focus on semantic SEO, EEAT, and topical authority, sharpened by studying Koray Tugberk Gubur's topical authority methodology in depth.

I believe good SEO isn't about gaming an algorithm, it's about structuring a site the way search engines are actually built to understand: as a network of entities, relationships, and intent. That's the lens I bring to every audit, every content brief, and every technical fix.
